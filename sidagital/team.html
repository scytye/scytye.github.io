Experience life at Si digital

We know how important it is to enjoy what you do and where you work, you spend more time there than you do at home after all. So we pride ourselves on having a fun, dedicated team and making the Si digital studio a lovely place to be.

Meet the Team

Steve Piper

Managing Director

Steve started the business back in 2001 from his home in Essex before moving down to sunny Southsea in 2007. By day, he’s here in the Si studio, focusing his time on developing and growing the agency, by night he’s a cooking addict, doting husband and father of two. Steve’s a fully fledged triathlete, with several under his belt now.

Alex Crooks

Lead Developer

Alex oversees all of our front and back end builds, ensuring they maintain the high quality we expect. He’s a developer with a keen eye for design and perfection. In his spare time, he loves touring on his motorbike, gaming, and when he can, throwing himself down snow covered mountains.

Oli Northam

Lead Technical

With strong experience in IT and backend development, Oli provides the nuts and bolts for all our web applications as well as ensuring that our technologies are all up-to-date and secure. In his free time, Oli likes to create experimental web apps, listen to his expansive collection of dodgy 90’s music and occasionally play Dance Dance Revolution at the arcade (don’t ask).

Tom O'Malley

Lead Designer

Tom heads up our design output, which started with our amazing rebrand. A pixel perfectionist, illustrator and ideas guy, he’s creative to the core. He loves getting inspired by travelling, exploring and taking photographs. When not busy being a designer, Tom can be found on the water or on a board; surfing, paddleboarding or longboarding the concrete waves.

Mike Watkins

Developer

Formerly a graduate from the University of Southampton, Mike provides brilliant technical support to our clients. He’s a meticulous coder who’s all about the details. When he isn't busy coding, Mike enjoys gaming, bee keeping and building Robots to take over the world.

Ash Stevens

Developer

Ash is one of our front end developers, you'll usually find him dabbling with the JavaScript flavour of the week. If he's not making things on the web, he's probably listening to a podcast about doing so, or trying to photograph something artsy to post on Instagram.

Jack Innes

Designer

Creating websites from a young age, Jack is a Photoshop guru with an eye for innovation and pixel perfect design. When he’s not designing or gaming, you can probably find Jack browsing Reddit or seeking inspiration on Dribbble.

Joe Cavalla

Project Account Manager

Joe is a highly experienced Project and Business Manager, with strong customer facing experience. Success as the Studio Head for one of the UK’s most established independent game developers has been complemented by significant achievements within Technology Transfer. Coupled with an enduring passion for the digital arts, Joe has continued to push boundaries - always striving to combine art and technology in the most innovative ways possible.

Richard Keen

Developer

Rich has gained several years of experience as a developer since his time at Chichester College. He can often be found playing with new web technologies, and is a big believer in writing beautiful code. A keen sailor, out of work you can find him navigating the Solent, or racking up the miles on his bike when the wind drops.

Sid

Office Robot

Sid heads up our lab and is the Si digital resident robot. His current role is to pick up balls under instruction from visitors across the world. When not playing games, Sid likes to relax with a can of WD-40.
